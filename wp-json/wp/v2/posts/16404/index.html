[vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]The Apache Software Foundation (ASF) held its annual convention, ApacheCon, September 29-October 1, 2020. Because of the pandemic, it was an all-virtual conference. Participants logged in and attended keynote speeches, track sessions, and informal hallway meetings from all around the world. The conference had a track in Mandarin, and sessions in Hindi.<\/p>\n

Attendance broke all previous records, with over three thousand people taking part<\/strong>. Over 80% were attending their first-ever ASF convention.<\/p>\n

There were 27 presentation tracks, the largest number ever for ApacheCon. Apache Royale was among them, with presenters in Pakistan, Spain, and Canada.[\/vc_column_text][\/vc_column][\/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column]
